Instruction Set for SpinCom VM

		00 INT #
1n LD Rn,#	01 SHL
2n LD A,Rn	02 SHR
3n LD Rn,A	03 INV
4n LD A,(Rn)	04 PUSH
5n LD (Rn),A	05 POP
6n LDB A,(Rn)	06 RET
7n LDB (Rn),A	07 EXT
8n ADD Rn	08 CALL #
9n SUB Rn	09 JMP #
An CMP Rn	0A LD A,#
Bn INC Rn	0B
Cn DEC Rn	0C Z
Dn AND Rn	0D NZ
En OR Rn	0E C
Fn XOR Rn	0F NC

Flags are zero and carry
Conditionals skip the next instruction (e.g. JNZ $FFFF would be NZ: JMP $FFFF)
Most operations affecting A affect ZF
INC and DEC also affect ZF
POP and LD A do not affect ZF
ADD, SUB, CMP, INC, DEC, SHL and SHR affect CF
The stack expands from 0..$FF
SP is not accessible
